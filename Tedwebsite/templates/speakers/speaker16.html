Ravindranath Ravi


Mr. Ravindranath Ravi is resolute and a focused social activist. He has selflessly devoted his entire life towards creation of training systems and successful livelihood options for the so-called backward society. His powerful talk in the 2016 edition of TEDxIITGuwahati shed light upon how he managed to turn flood-ravaged regions of Assam into successful villages by creating new and alternative livelihoods.
